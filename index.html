What’s the DHF?

The DIY Healer’s Flea is an all ages market with special areas for learning and healing using a creative, communal, and self-guided approach. It is free to attend, although vendors and service providers must first register to reserve commercial space at the event.

Our Fleas offer a way for people working in various methods of healing to come together and share their practices with fellow healers as well as the general public. We believe in fostering creative approaches, self-guided practices, and communal, mutual self-education. The Flea is a combination of vendors, speakers, healers, and you!
