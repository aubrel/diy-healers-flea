The DIY Healers Flea

Participant Registration

Interested in vending, speaking, or healing at the next DHF? Wonderful! We would love to have you. First, take a look at the rules and guidelines below, and please keep in mind that by applying for a participant position, you are agreeing to the following terms as we have outlined them.

For All Participants

  1. By applying for the DHF, you agree to all stipulations and requests made by the host venue. Any behavior deemed disrespectful or that goes against the policies of the host venue will result in a permanent ban from participating in future fleas, so make sure you review the guidelines provided by the host venue, which are included on the home page with the information for the next upcoming flea.
  2. We are strictly an anti-racist, pro-queer, radically oriented event and we hold space for that. Any behavior that goes against this will result in a ban.
  3. All participants must be engaged in a practice of healing, mending, or repair in some respect. We are open to hearing however you define this!

For Vendors

The role of Vendor is for individuals who want to sell their own handmade wares or basic services at the Flea. If you are more interested in providing services, take a look at the Healer guidelines below.

  1. Vendors must pay a non-refundable table fee of $45.00 USD.
  2. Vendors must show up one hour before the beginning of the Flea.
  3. Vendors must bring their own tables, which must not be larger than 48" in length by 24" in width.
  4. Vendors must bring any and all the materials they will need.
  5. Vendors' tables must not be larger than 48" in length by 24" in width.
  6. Vendors may keep 100% of their profits.

Proceed to the Vendor Registration Form.

For Speakers

The role of Speaker is for individuals who want to give a talk about or lead a brief workshop on a topic that they know a bit about. A talk does not need to provide materials for demonstration purposes, whereas a workshop does.

  1. All talks must be a minimum of 30 minutes and a maximum of 60 minutes. Workshops must be a minimum of 45 minutes and a maximum of 90 minutes.
  2. Speakers are not required to pay a fee, however, all speaking events must be offered freely to all attendees of the Flea.
  3. Speakers may request donations for their talk.
  4. Speakers are encouraged to make announcements for their upcoming workshops or events.

Proceed to the Speaker Registration Form.

For Healers

The role of Healer is for individuals who have a a consistent, dedicated practice of healing, mending, or repair in some capacity, and who would like to offer a sample of their work to the public.

  1. Healers must pay a fee equal to 20% of what they make during the Flea.
  2. Healers may provide services for free, or they may request fees up to $45.00 USD per healing session. This may also be requested on a sliding scale basis; that choice is up to the Healer.
  3. Healers are completely responsible for the legalities regarding their practices, and are encouraged to inform attendees of this and/or to advise them that their agreeing to a healing practice comes at their own risk. Neither the host venue nor the Flea organizers are responsible for the results of any healing practices.
  4. Healers must ask for consent from all participants before engaging in any healing practices and must allow clients to opt out at any time. Failure to abide by this guideline will result in a permanent ban and potential legal action.

Proceed to the Healer Registration Form.
